üèÜ COMPETITIVE EXAMS

NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)

  • Mechanics: Kinematics, Laws of Motion, Work Energy Power, Rotational Motion, Gravitation
  • Properties of Matter: Elasticity, Surface Tension, Viscosity, Thermal Properties
  • Thermodynamics: Laws of Thermodynamics, Kinetic Theory, Heat Transfer
  • Oscillations & Waves: SHM, Wave Motion, Sound Waves, Doppler Effect
  • Electrostatics: Coulomb's Law, Electric Field, Potential, Capacitance
  • Current Electricity: Ohm's Law, Kirchhoff's Laws, Electrical Instruments
  • Magnetism: Magnetic Field, Electromagnetic Induction, AC Circuits
  • Optics: Reflection, Refraction, Optical Instruments, Wave Optics
  • Modern Physics: Photoelectric Effect, Bohr's Model, Nuclear Physics

JEE (Joint Entrance Examination)

  • Mechanics: Advanced Kinematics, Complex Force Analysis, Rotational Dynamics
  • Fluid Mechanics: Bernoulli's Theorem, Viscous Flow, Surface Phenomena
  • Heat & Thermodynamics: Advanced Thermodynamic Processes, Heat Engines
  • Simple Harmonic Motion: Coupled Oscillations, Damped & Forced Oscillations
  • Waves: Interference, Diffraction, Polarization, Electromagnetic Waves
  • Electrostatics: Complex Electric Field Problems, Dielectrics
  • Current Electricity: Network Analysis, Measuring Instruments
  • Magnetism: Advanced Electromagnetic Induction, LCR Circuits
  • Optics: Advanced Ray Optics, Wave Optics, Optical Instruments
  • Modern Physics: Quantum Mechanics, Radioactivity, Nuclear Reactions

MHT-CET (Maharashtra Common Entrance Test)

  • 11th Standard Topics: All Maharashtra Board 11th Physics chapters
  • 12th Standard Topics: All Maharashtra Board 12th Physics chapters
  • Emphasis Areas: Rotational Dynamics, Wave Motion, Current Electricity
  • Special Focus: Numerical Problem Solving, Application-based Questions
  • Practical Applications: Real-world Physics Problems
  • Mathematical Physics: Vector Analysis, Differential Equations
